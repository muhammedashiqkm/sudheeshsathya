About Me

Hi, I'm Sudheesh S — a finance professional and lifelong learner passionate about understanding how money, markets, and human behavior connect.

My journey began in India, where I studied commerce and worked in Delhi before moving to Dubai, where I now work as a Market Research Analyst. Along the way, I've cleared CFA Levels I and II and am preparing for Level III (2026), focusing on valuation, equity research, and global markets.

This website is where I share my thoughts on finance, psychology, and personal growth — blending analytical insight with real-world reflection. Some posts dive into investing and business models; others explore ideas about mindset, purpose, and decision-making.

I created this space to learn in public — to grow, reflect, and connect with others who are curious about how money, meaning, and mindset shape the world we live in.
